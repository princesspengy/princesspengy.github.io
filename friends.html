friends of the site

this page will be where I tell you about my friends and the cool stuff they get up to on the internet!! you should go check em out!!

O.D. the Cryptid

my best buddy!! they're an artist, habitual streamer, and general little freak who has some of the best character designs and general artistic sensibilities in the business. they're scattered across a lot of different places on the internet nowadays, which you can all find on their website!

SleepyGayCutie

my wonderful wonderful girlfriend, a cartoonist by trade with a penchant for cute girls in glasses getting hypnotized and otherwise mind controlled in an endless variety of ways! find all of her art and stuff on bluesky (18+)!

Bee Braun

develops games!! we talk about video games a whole ton and despite making games that might seem outwardly simplistic, she has such an intense thoughtfulness towards the medium that makes her stuff an absolute delight. she has a website you can visit here!

inverseaxolotl

webcomic author, animator, and clownfucker nonpareil. currently working on all kinds of stories about people being horrible disasters and making a lot of other art on the side, which you can see most of on instagram! she also has a sideblog where she draws kinky porny goodness of all kinds over on bluesky (18+)!

Utilitymonstergirl

the finest purveyor I know of queer media that chooses violence on purpose, alexi is a webcomic artist who blends comedy, pornography, transgressiveness, and philosophy into a big garishly colored soup that still comes out tasting absolutely delicious. check out her blog!

GigglingGoblin

a writer of joyously devilish hypnosmut, and one of my biggest inspirations for my own writing. come for the seductive supernatural beauties and top-class erotica, stay for the evocative and thought-provoking worldbuilding! read her work on readonlymind (18+), and other miscellany on bluesky (18+)!!

sieversii.

makes kickass electronic music, influenced by edm of the 2010s (she listened to a lot of monstercat releases back in the day) among a few other things. also she does her own vocals and in my extremely unbiased opinion it's like being serenaded by an angel? listen to her newest releases on bandcamp!

Ara

another lewd goblin friend of mine!! ara is known for her groundbreaking work in the fields of "what if there were eggs in people" and "what if this huge menacing looking monster actually fucks like a champion". these days she's been migrating over to bluesky (18+) and you should follow it for all kinds of kinky fun!

SKCB

also known as "eskie"! she's a writer for tabletop rpgs, participating in a writing co-op, working with various publishers, and working on her own homemade system! keep up with her latest work on patreon!
