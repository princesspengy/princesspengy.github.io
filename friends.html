friends of the site

this page will be where I tell you about my friends and the cool stuff they get up to on the internet!! you should go check em out!!

O.D. the Cryptid

my best buddy!! they're an artist, habitual streamer, and general little freak who has some of the best character designs and general artistic sensibilities in the business. they're scattered across a lot of different places on the internet these days, which you can all find on their website!

GigglingGoblin

a writer of joyously devilish hypnosmut, and one of my biggest inspirations for my own writing. come for the seductive supernatural beauties and top-class erotica, stay for the evocative and thought-provoking worldbuilding! read her work on readonlymind (18+), and other miscellany on bluesky (18+)!!

SleepyGayCutie

my wonderful wonderful girlfriend, a cartoonist by trade with a penchant for cute girls in glasses getting hypnotized and otherwise mind controlled in an endless variety of ways! she's partnered with the abovementioned gigglinggoblin to provide the occasional bit of art, and she's drawing her own stuff over on bluesky (18+)!

Bee Braun

develops games!! we talk about video games a whole ton and despite making games that might seem outwardly simplistic, they have such an intense thoughtfulness towards the medium that makes their stuff an absolute delight. they have a website you can visit here!

Utilitymonstergirl

the finest purveyor I know of queer media that chooses violence on purpose, alexi is a webcomic artist who blends comedy, pornography, transgressiveness, and philosophy into a big garishly colored soup that still comes out tasting absolutely delicious. check out her her blog!

inverseaxolotl

webcomic author, animator, and clownfucker nonpareil. currently working on all kinds of stories about people being horrible disasters and making a lot of other art on the side, which you can see most of on instagram! she also has a sideblog where she draws kinky porny goodness of all kinds over on bluesky (18+)!

sieversii.

makes kickass electronic music, influenced by edm of the 2010s (she listened to a lot of monstercat releases back in the day) among a few other things. also she does her own vocals and in my extremely unbiased opinion it's like being serenaded by an angel? listen to her newest releases on bandcamp!

SKCB

also known as "eskie"! she's a writer for tabletop rpgs, both participating in a writing co-op as well as working on her own homemade system! she's also been trying out at game development lately, with a project in the works I'm super excited about. keep up with her latest work on patreon!
